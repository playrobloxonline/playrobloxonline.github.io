About This Game

Raft Wars Unblocked is an exciting game where you can test your skills and have fun. The objective is to score as many points as possible while avoiding obstacles and completing challenges.

With intuitive controls and progressively difficult levels, this game offers hours of entertainment for players of all skill levels.

Controls

  • W or ↑ - Move Up
  • A or ← - Move Left
  • S or ↓ - Move Down
  • D or → - Move Right
  • Space - Action / Jump
